Drink Water before Meals

The optimum time to drink water is roughly half an hour before meals. It leads to faster metabolism and consequently more calorie burn.

Faster metabolism means that the calories you consume are burnt off quicker at rest as well as during activity. So, if you are looking to lose some weight, a healthy way to achieve that is to try and boost your metabolism naturally.

Additionally, drinking around 8.5 glasses of water in a day overall can lead to 24 – 30% increase in metabolism.

In terms of calories, a 24 – 30% metabolism increase means that you burn around 96 calories by regulating your water intake alone.

Since our body needs water anyway to stay healthy, even if metabolism isn’t affected greatly you will nevertheless see improvements in general health. It is also great for skin.
