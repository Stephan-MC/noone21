Website Description

This is a multi-vendor marketplace for professionals from the media and publishing industry. The platform was built to provide creatives direct access to each other so that employers can find the right talent for their projects and professionals and talent can get the exposure they need.

By eliminating the need for agencies and third parties, this marketplace makes the hiring process a lot more efficient.
