Use of Cookies

A cookie is a file that is saved on to the hard drive of a user’s computer for the purpose of collecting information. There are two types of cookies that are used by most platforms at the moment and which we use too. There are persistent cookies that have a longer life and these are used for the purpose of saving your password and username as well as to keep track of whether a user is continuing to comply with the platform policies. The second type is the session ID cookie which is only valid and in use during a session. As soon as a session expires, the cookie expires too. This type of cookie is used to collect information about a user’s activities and interaction with the website during a session. This data pertais to the user’s interaction with the website only, is statistical in nature and derived from the server web logs.

The purpose behind data collection is to improve user experience and optimize it for their future visits.Data collected through cookies is not of the nature that can be used to identify visitors of the website.

Data collected may include date and time of a user’s visit, IP address, browser version, and the operating system on the device they’re using.
